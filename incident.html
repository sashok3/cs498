What do we see?

From the data we observe that South East Asia faces an increase in the number of incident cases over the years and Africa witness an increase in number of cases by more than 1 million. Western Pacific is the only region that observes an actual decrease in number of cases while Eastern Mediterranean, Americas and Europe don't undergo much of a change.
